About Queen's River

The tranquility of this town is shattered when a woman is kidnapped under mysterious circumstances. You are plunged into a world of intrigue and deceit. As you progress through the game, you interact with friends and acquaintances of the victim, each harboring their own secrets and motivations. The key to solving this enigma lies in your ability to connect the victim's turbulent past with the current events unfolding in Queen's River

A Journey of Discovery

This game is not just a race against time to save the victim, but also a journey through the complexity of human relationships and dark secrets. Each clue unearthed brings you a step closer to the truth, but also plunges you deeper into a labyrinth of lies and half-truths.

Hacker Skills

In "Queen's River", your hacking skills are essential to solve a mystery intertwined between the digital and the real. Decipher codes, infiltrate secure systems, and manipulate technology to access hidden clues. Face hacking challenges that test your wits, balancing risk with the reward of crucial information.
